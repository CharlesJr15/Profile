About Me

After graduation in 2023, I decided to advance my coding skills as Software Development student at Danfred Computer Engineering Ltd, where I learnt how to build modern, responsive, and scalable web applications. My goal is to become a full-stack developer, contribute to open-source projects, and one day build software that solves real problems in Africa and beyond, this is the initiative behind the establishment of CJTechs.
